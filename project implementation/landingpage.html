About the DISC Model

The DISC model is a way to make sense of how people behave and deal with others. It breaks things down into four main types Dominance, Influence, Steadiness, and Conscientiousness. Each one shows a different style, some people are more direct and focused on results, others are more social and expressive, some like things calm and predictable and others pay close attention to details and rules. It’s often used at work to help people communicate better, understand each other, and work together more smoothly.
